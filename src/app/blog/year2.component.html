More levels with newly introduced Story Packs

In Year 2 Lego Dimensions introduces Story Packs. Story Packs are something inbetween a Level Pack and a full-blown Lego videogame. Each Story Pack is said to contain six levels of gameplay where you can replay some of the major plot scenes of the movie.
